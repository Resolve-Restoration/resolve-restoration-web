Our Services

Fire Damage

Right after the smoke clears out, we are the ones who are next to restore your possessions, removing smoke soot and corrosive chemicals from every surface, making sure to leave it as it was.

Water Damage

Whether it is a broken pipe, faulty appliances or water intrusion, we are here for it. With our expertise on strata, we know how important it is to mitigate the problem quickly and consider it resolved.

Mold Remediation

Mould and Odor accumulated indoor and outdoor from all type of surfaces, affecting your health and the structure of your home. You can consider it resolved with us sanitizing the area and leaving it healthy.

Vandalism

Immediate action is required after an incident that can cause significant damage over time and that’s why we are here: to restore the damage, remove debris and clean it up. Leaving it resolved.

Contents Packaging and Assessment

Contents evaluation and listing services, packing and inventory, moving and sorting, complete contents removal and temporary storage services.

Leak Investigation

As soon as you notice the elevated price on your water bill, from toilets to underground pipes and behind walls, we are ready to detect and restore the leak, mitigating damages and leaving it resolved.

Property Preventatives

Installation of preventive measures from unexpected damage in residential and commercial areas: ballard, enclosures, curbs, cages, covers, line painting speed bumps and more.

Building Envelope Inspection and Repairs

We offer inspections for residential and commercial areas alongside repairs needed from the inspections: siding, fascia, gutters, hand rails, decks, windows and more.

Concrete Repairs and Replacement

Quality work with concrete structures repairs and replacement. Spalling, deterioration, deficiencies and concrete injection repairs improving the appereance of your property leaving it resolved.

About Us

Resolve Restoration gets it resolved. We focus on assisting you at every level, from the emergency response to the mitigating and aftermath repair processes. We pride ourselves on responding to our clients the way we would if these were our very own homes. We provide services for both strata and commercial properties in the Lower Mainland. During emergency situations, time is of the essence, and we focus on mitigating damages as quickly as possible. Counting with our team means you do not need to worry about every aspect of the emergency process. Our team is well-versed in the processes during and in the aftermath of a loss. Working with industry professionals to accommodate your needs will ensure your experience is as smooth and straightforward as it can ever be.

We're Fast

In an emergency situation, time is of the essence and responsiveness is crucial. Having crews around the clock means we will be able to attend to your call immediately and able to dispatch crews your way faster than ever.

We're Professional

We are professionals and make a big effort to keep our clients informed at all times. Emergency situations can be stressful, but you can trust our team to handle all aspects of the emergency in the most professional way.

We're Everywhere

We service the full Lower Mainland. We strategically have centralized locations to have better and quick access to our equipment so our crews can be on the go and therefore, better attend to your needs.

Resolve Get it Resolved.

A loss can happen at any given time; this is when Resolve resolves any situation. We dispatch the most suitable staff needed for the right situation and are available 24/7.

We understand strata

At the strata level, we step into each property with a collaborative approach. We understand strata very well, Understanding the various layers of strata allows us to be accountable to all owners, property manager representatives, strata councils, and developers.

Our approach

It is important for us to keep a flexible approach when stepping into each resident’s homes or common areas during the findings of damages in order to protect the privacy and comfort of the residents.
